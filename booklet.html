A Journey of Learning and Fun!

Dive into our booklet for an enriching experience where you'll not only explore the secrets of environmental conservation but also engage in exciting activities. From unraveling the starting story to sorting materials, learning English, and solving math tasks, this booklet promises a journey filled with knowledge and fun!

Meet Olaf

Olaf, your trusty companion, leads you through the pages of the booklet. Follow his guidance as he unveils the secrets of environmental conservation, making the learning journey both engaging and memorable..

Discovery by the Lake

Join the adventure as kids stumble upon a polluted lake and seek guidance from Olaf to unravel the mysteries of environmental conservation.

Material Sorting Game

Engage in a fun and educational game where kids learn to differentiate materials and sort them correctly, contributing to the cleanliness of the environment..

Global Investigation

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean.

Design Your Legacy

Unleash your creativity and leave a mark on the DPG legacy. Draw your own interpretation of the DPG logo, showcasing your unique vision for a sustainable future.

Champions of the Clear Waters

Behold the beauty of the now-clean lake, a testament to your dedication and eco-friendly efforts. Receive your well-deserved certificate for completing all the games and becoming a true champion in saving the lake.
